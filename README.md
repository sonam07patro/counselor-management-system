Counselor Management System (CMS)

Logic Test

1. Maximum Students Sarah Can Handle

Sarah works 6 days per week (Tuesday–Sunday).
Monday is OFF.

Assuming 8 working hours per day.

Weekly hours = 6 × 8 = 48

Monthly hours = 48 × 4 = 192

Each student requires:
3 sessions per month
1 hour per session

Total = 3 hours per student

Maximum students = 192 / 3 = 64 students
